About Us

Empowering Lives Through Kindness and Sustainability

Founded in August 2020 by Reesha Ahmed, Share Your Closet (SYC) began as a small initiative to share clothes with those in need. Today, it stands as a national youth-led organization that uplifts communities through clothing donations, food drives, medical aid, and educational support.

Our mission is simple — to bridge social and economic gaps by turning excess into opportunity. We collect, recycle, and repurpose essentials to ensure that every person, regardless of background, can live with dignity and hope.

Over the years, SYC has expanded beyond clothing. We now provide medical support to rural families, educational resources to street children, fresh meals to the hungry, and rescue treatment for street animals. Every act of service is a step toward a fairer, more compassionate Bangladesh.

Our Vision

A Bangladesh where every individual lives with dignity, access to essentials, and the opportunity to thrive.

Our Mission

To empower lives through sustainable donation systems, awareness, and youth-led volunteerism across clothing, food, healthcare, and education.

“We believe every person deserves warmth, dignity, and hope. Together, we can turn small acts of kindness into a world of change.”

— Reesha Ahmed, Founder

Our Journey

Milestones of Hope and Humanity

From one simple idea to a nationwide movement — see how Share Your Closet has grown year by year.

2020 — The Beginning

Founded by Reesha Ahmed during the pandemic, Share Your Closet began with a small team distributing clothes to underprivileged families in Dhaka. What started as a local act of kindness grew into a purpose-driven vision.

2021 — Expanding Beyond Clothing

Introduced food and hygiene drives to reach rural communities. Partnered with volunteers and schools to start street children education sessions and raise awareness for menstrual hygiene.

2022 — Building Community Support

Launched large-scale winter campaigns and health checkups in remote areas. Began collecting data to improve distribution efficiency and ensure transparency.

2023 — Environmental and Animal Care Initiatives

Expanded our work to include tree-planting programs and street animal rescue and treatment. Strengthened our volunteer network with over 100+ active members across Bangladesh.

2024 — Collaboration and Impact

Partnered with local organizations to introduce community education and health programs. Implemented eco-friendly donation systems focusing on recycling and sustainability.

2025 — The Future We’re Building

Moving toward digital transparency and nationwide operations. Plans include online donation tracking, volunteer training programs, and expanding our presence beyond Bangladesh.

Child Education

Purpose: Ensure street children receive quality education and learning opportunities.

Volunteer Activities:

  • Teaching literacy and numeracy in informal classrooms.
  • Creating engaging educational materials and activities.
  • Mentoring children for personal and emotional development.
  • Organizing workshops, reading sessions, and skill-building activities.

Impact: Improves literacy, builds confidence, and opens future opportunities for vulnerable children.

Medical Treatment

Purpose: Provide essential healthcare access to underprivileged populations.

Volunteer Activities:

  • Assisting in medical camps and health check-up drives.
  • Coordinating patient registration and queue management.
  • Educating communities about hygiene, nutrition, and preventive care.
  • Supporting vaccination and first-aid programs.

Impact: Reduces health risks, spreads awareness, and improves overall community wellbeing.

Fresh Food

Purpose: Engage directly with local communities to address their immediate needs.

Volunteer Activities:

  • Organizing awareness campaigns on social issues (health, hygiene, education).
  • Coordinating community events and drives.
  • Mentoring and supporting local youth initiatives.
  • Assisting in distribution of essential items like food, clothes, and supplies.

Impact: Strengthens community ties, empowers residents, and raises awareness of social challenges.
